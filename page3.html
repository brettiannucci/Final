Nooch Entertainment

Brett Iannucci

Artist Manager & CEO

After accidentally stumbling upon an old audio console in his high school Brett Iannucci has been fascinated with both the production side and business side of music ever since. Once he graduated high school he attended Millersville University's Tell School Of Music in 2017 majoring in Music Industry focusing primarily on the business side of the industry. From then on Brett has had the opportunity to learn the ins and outs of the music industry through professionals as well as work with large artists like Third Eye Blind, Young The Giant, Lee Laamaj, Jay Cass, The Wonder Years and many more amazing groups. Not only did Brett help establish and manage the Philadelphia Label, Music Of The Covenant, with CEO and artist Aaron "Gobbana"Pleasant but he has also interned for the LA based tech and social record company, Ryse Up, where he worked as an A&R Representative dealing with a number of different and unique clients while also helping establish and co-lead their label, RYSE Music. Today, Brett studies Music Business Entrepreneurship and Technology at The University Of The Arts in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania while also working closely with Ryse Up as their Distribution Coordinator.
